# Internal Inspection Checklist — English Translation (2017 E.C. / 2025 G.C.)

**Internal Inspection Checklist for Primary and Middle Level Non-Government Schools**

Publisher: Ministry of Education, Ethiopia | February 2025 (ካቲት 2017 E.C.)

Framework code: `MOE_NG_PRIMARY_MIDDLE_2017`

Scoring: Performance rating per criterion: 0–3

## Introduction

For primary and middle level schools, collecting timely and reliable information from the institution using 28 standards, 100 indicators, and 304 criteria—organized under Input, Process, and Output—is essential to identify gaps, provide appropriate support, strengthen existing good practices, and sustain improvement. On this basis, non-government primary and middle schools can be rated against the three domains, recognize strengths alongside areas for improvement, and provide feedback to the institution and relevant stakeholders.

## Framework Structure

| Domain | Weight | Sections |
|--------|--------|----------|
| Input | 20% | Facilities and Infrastructure (8%); Comfortable Environment (4%); Finance and Human Resources (8%) |
| Process | 60% | School Leadership and Administration — Leadership Capacity (10%); Teaching Process and Motivation (8%); School Administration (5%); Teaching and Learning — Learning (5%); Teaching (11%); Collaborative Work (6%); School, Parent and Community Relations and Partnership (10%) |
| Output | 20% | Quality Education Participation (13%); Improved School Culture (7%) |
